Welcome! I am a Senior Economist at Amazon and hold a Ph.D. in Economics from Brown University. My research uses causal inference, economic modeling, and machine learning to study labor markets, urban economics, and public policy.

I have extensive experience analyzing large-scale data — from building infrastructure to data gathering, warehousing, and analysis — using R, Python, Stata, QGIS, and SQL.
